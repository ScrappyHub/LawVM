# Who LawVM Is For

LawVM is for people who want local, deterministic policy decisions instead of hidden app logic.

## Good fits

- developers adding allow/deny checks to scripts or tools
- small teams that want policy files in a repo
- automation workflows that need local guardrails
- AI/tooling experiments where an action should be checked before execution
- security-minded users who want stable reason codes instead of vague failures

## Not a good fit yet

- enterprise identity management replacement
- cloud authorization service replacement
- audited compliance product
- full policy language with complex conditions
- networked policy distribution system

## Plain-English pitch

LawVM answers: “Given this policy and this request, should this action be allowed?”
